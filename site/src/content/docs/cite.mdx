If you reference CAAIL or build on its curated content, please cite the repository. CAAIL is
archived on Zenodo with a concept DOI that always resolves to the latest version:

[![DOI](https://zenodo.org/badge/DOI/10.5281/zenodo.20295590.svg)](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20295590)

On GitHub, the **“Cite this repository”** button in the sidebar generates these same citations from
the [`CITATION.cff`](https://github.com/tucca-cellag/caail/blob/main/CITATION.cff) at the repository
root.

## Citing a specific resource

When you cite a specific catalogued resource — a paper, a software tool, a dataset — cite the
**original source** directly rather than CAAIL. The DOIs and canonical URLs in the
[Papers Explorer](/caail/papers/explorer/), [Software](/caail/software/),
[Databases](/caail/databases/), and the per-species [Datasets](/caail/datasets/readme/) pages are
authoritative. Cite CAAIL when you are referring to the library itself — its organization, its
matrix, or the curated collection as a whole.
