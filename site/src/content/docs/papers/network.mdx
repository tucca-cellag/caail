Each node is a paper from the [Papers Explorer](/caail/papers/explorer/); an edge joins two
papers that **share an author**, surfacing research lineages and collaborator clusters the matrix
doesn't show. Node size reflects how many co-authorship links a paper has; color marks its primary
research area. Filter by area, toggle unconnected papers, and click a node for its details.
