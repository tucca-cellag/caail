Each node is a paper from the [Papers Explorer](/caail/papers/explorer/). Use the toggle to switch
how edges are drawn:

- **Shared author** — an undirected edge joins two papers that share an author, surfacing research
  lineages and collaborator clusters the matrix doesn't show.
- **Citation** — a directed edge runs from a paper to one it cites (the arrow points at the cited
  paper), surfacing the field's intellectual structure. Citation links are derived from
  [OpenAlex](https://openalex.org/) and shown only between papers both held in CAAIL.

Node size reflects how connected a paper is in the active mode; color marks its primary research
area. Filter by area, toggle unconnected papers, and click a node for its details.
